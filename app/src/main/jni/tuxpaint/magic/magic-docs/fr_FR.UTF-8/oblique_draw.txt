                   Outil 'Magie' de Tux Paint : Oblique Draw

                          ### Groupe: Projections ###

           ## Auteur : Bill Kendrick <bill@newbreedsoftware.com> ##

Click and drag to draw in an oblique projection. Lines will only go vertically,
horizontally, and at a chosen angle. Use "Oblique Select" to adjust the angle.
In "beginner" mode, two "Oblique Draw" tools will be available, one with with
the receding axis angle going right, and one with it going left.

Cet outil permet de travailler en mode à main levée.

Cet outil permet de travailler avec plusieurs tailles.

Cet outil permet de choisir des couleurs.

This tool is not available with "complexity" (expertise level) set to 'Novice'.

Voir aussi : Dessin 1 point, Choix 1 point, Dessin 2 points, Choix 2 points,
Dessin à 3-Point, Choix 3-Point, Isometric Line, Dimetric Select, Dimetric Draw
, Trimetric Select, Trimetric Draw, Oblique Select, & Perspective.
