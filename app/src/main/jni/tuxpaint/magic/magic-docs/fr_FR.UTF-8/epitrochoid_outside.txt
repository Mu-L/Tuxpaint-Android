               Outil 'Magie' de Tux Paint : Epitrochoid Outside

                              Groupe: Artistique

              Auteur : Bill Kendrick <bill@newbreedsoftware.com>

Click to place the center of an epitrochoid curve, then drag left/right to
adjust the radius of a fixed circle, and up/down to adjust the radius of the
circle that will roll along its outside to produce the drawing. The "pen" that
paints the curve will be on the outside of the rolling circle.

Cet outil permet de travailler en mode à main levée.

Cet outil permet de choisir des couleurs.

Voir aussi : Epitrochoid, Epitrochoid Inside, Epitrochoid Edge, Hypotrochoid,
Hypotrochoid Inside, Hypotrochoid Edge, Hypotrochoid Outside, Ficelles dans les
coins, Ficelles sur les bords, & Ficelles V.
