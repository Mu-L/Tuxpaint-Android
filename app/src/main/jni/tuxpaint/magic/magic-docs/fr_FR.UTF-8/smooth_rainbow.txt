                    Tux Paint "Magic" Tool: Arc-en-ciel lent

                                Group: Painting

               Author: Bill Kendrick <bill@newbreedsoftware.com>

   This is similar to Rainbow magic tool — as you move the mouse around, it
   cycles through a spectrum of colors — except the colors blend smoothly as
   you draw.

   This tool is operated in a freehand fashion.

   This tool offers multiple size settings.

   See also: Arc-en-ciel & Rainbow Cycle.
